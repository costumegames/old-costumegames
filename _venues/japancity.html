With a daily footfall of one hundred thousand, the Old Steine Gardens are one of the most prominent locations Brighton has to offer. For the period of the festival, Japan City will play host to a number of venues including the famous MOSHIMO Supper Club, a picnic-style Cinema Tent, the Festival Bar, and a retail area for stalls.
